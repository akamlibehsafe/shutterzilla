About ShutterZilla

ShutterZilla is a platform designed for vintage camera enthusiasts. We provide modern tools to help you discover, collect, and manage your film camera collection with ease.

Whether you're a seasoned collector hunting for rare finds or just starting your journey into the world of analog photography, ShutterZilla has the tools you need.

Our Apps

ShutterZilla Scraper

Search and discover vintage cameras across multiple online marketplaces. Our scraper aggregates listings from Mercari, Buyee, eBay USA, and JD Direct Auctions, giving you a comprehensive view of what's available. Set up saved searches with custom filters and get notified when new cameras matching your criteria are listed.

ShutterZilla Collection

Organize and manage your personal camera collection. Track cameras you own, maintain a wishlist of cameras you're looking for, and keep detailed records including purchase price, condition, and notes. View statistics about your collection and watch its value over time.

Features

  • Multi-Source Search - Search across multiple marketplaces simultaneously
  • Smart Filters - Filter by brand, price range, condition, and source
  • Saved Searches - Save your search criteria and get notified of new listings
  • Collection Management - Track owned cameras and wishlist items
  • Statistics & Insights - Visualize your collection's composition and value
  • Responsive Design - Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices

Our Mission

We believe that vintage cameras deserve to be preserved, used, and appreciated. Our mission is to connect collectors with the cameras they're looking for and help them maintain their collections with modern, easy-to-use tools.

ShutterZilla was built by photographers and collectors who understand the joy of finding that perfect camera and the satisfaction of building a meaningful collection.
